We've officially launched **PlanFlow**, AIAN's production management (MES) solution. Orders automatically generate plans, and floor results sync back in real time.

## Key features

- Order → plan automation
- Digital handling from work orders to completion reports
- Delivery, yield, and bottlenecks on one screen

Built to apply directly on the floor, it also connects to your existing ERP via API. Reach out through the contact form to learn more.
